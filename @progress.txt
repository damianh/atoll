  - Static segments: exact match (case-insensitive)

- RouteMatchResult: matched entry + extracted parameters dictionary (case-insensitive keys)

**Key discoveries for Phase 11:**
- `RenderContext.RenderAsync(RenderFragment)` is the correct way to render a fragment inside a ComponentDelegate — it delegates to `fragment.RenderAsync(_destination)` internally
- `ClientDirectiveType` enum is in `Atoll.Core.Instructions` namespace, NOT `Atoll.Core.Islands`
- `RenderPageAsync(ComponentDelegate)` wraps the delegate in a RenderContext; to render a RenderFragment inside it, use `ctx.RenderAsync(fragment)` not `fragment.RenderAsync(ctx)`
- AtollRequestHandler.HandlePageAsync only builds props from route parameters — pages needing DI services (like CollectionQuery) must receive them via direct rendering, not through the middleware
